About LiftShift

LiftShift is a free, privacy-focused workout analytics dashboard. It turns workout logs into charts and definitions you can actually interpret.

What LiftShift does

  • Imports workout data from supported apps (Hevy, Strong, Lyfta).
  • Processes analytics locally in your browser.
  • Summarizes progress with dashboards for training volume, personal records (PRs), exercise history, and muscle emphasis.

What LiftShift does not do

  • It is not a workout plan generator or coaching program.
  • It is not medical advice.
  • It does not require you to upload your full training history to a LiftShift-owned database.

How to interpret the charts

LiftShift tries to make the “why” explicit. When a chart shows a trend or a label, it is based only on the data you imported. If your logging is inconsistent (exercise names, units, missing sets), the charts will reflect that.
