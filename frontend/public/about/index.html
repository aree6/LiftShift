About LiftShift - Best Free Workout Analytics Dashboard Add-On

LiftShift is the best free workout analytics dashboard add-on for fitness tracking apps. It transforms workout logs from Hevy, Strong, Lyfta and other fitness apps into professional analytics dashboards with advanced data visualization and insights.

What LiftShift does - Advanced Analytics Dashboard

  • Imports workout data from supported fitness apps (Hevy, Strong, Lyfta).
  • Creates advanced analytics dashboards with professional data visualization.
  • Processes all analytics locally in your browser for maximum privacy.
  • Provides dashboard features for training volume analysis, personal records tracking, exercise history, and muscle emphasis.
  • Works as a dashboard add-on to enhance existing workout logging apps.
  • Offers free analytics dashboard features comparable to paid fitness platforms.

What LiftShift does not do

  • It is not a workout plan generator or fitness coaching program.
  • It is not medical advice or nutrition tracking.
  • It does not require you to upload your full training history to a LiftShift-owned database.
  • It is not a replacement for workout logging apps (it enhances them with analytics dashboards).
  • It does not charge subscription fees for advanced dashboard features.

How to interpret the charts

LiftShift tries to make the “why” explicit. When a chart shows a trend or a label, it is based only on the data you imported. If your logging is inconsistent (exercise names, units, missing sets), the charts will reflect that.
