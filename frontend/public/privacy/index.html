Privacy

LiftShift is designed to work without sending your full training history to a LiftShift-owned database. The goal is: you get analytics, without giving up control of your data.

What LiftShift processes

  • Workout logs you import (CSV) or sync via supported integrations.
  • Derived analytics such as training volume, PRs, and trend summaries.

Where your data is processed

Analytics are computed locally in your browser. This includes charts, summaries, and per-exercise history.

What LiftShift stores

  • Data you choose to keep in your browser (for example via local storage) so the app can load faster next time.
  • Preferences (such as weight units and body map settings).

What LiftShift does not store on a LiftShift server

  • Your complete workout history as an account profile.
  • Your derived analytics as a server-side dataset.
